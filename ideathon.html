What's a dApp? What's This Ideathon About?

Decentralized Applications (dApps) are the building blocks of Web3, running on blockchain technology to offer transparency, security, and user empowerment. Think beyond traditional apps – how can you leverage dApps to solve real-world problems or create entirely new experiences in areas like finance (DeFi), digital ownership (NFTs), gaming, social networks, and more?

This Ideathon is your chance to dive deep into the world of Web3. We're calling on all university innovators across India to brainstorm, conceptualize, and present dApp ideas that have the potential to make a real impact. No coding is required for submission – just your brilliant concepts and a clear vision!

How to Participate & What We're Looking For

Submission Process

Ready to submit your groundbreaking idea? Simply fill out the submission form below with your dApp concept.

No prototypes or demos are required at this stage – we're looking for innovative and well-thought-out ideas!

Ensure all required fields in the form are completed thoroughly to give your idea the best chance.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity & Innovation (40%): How unique and original is the idea? Does it push the boundaries of current Web3 applications?
  • Impact & Potential (30%): How significant is the problem it solves or the value it creates? Who benefits and how?
  • Feasibility (30%): How practical is the idea? Can it realistically be developed (especially with a tool like Cortexea)?
